I am a research associate at the University of Cambridge working with Prof. Cecilia Mascolo. My work enables efficient ML and AI to make the most out of real-world data (e.g. timeseries, audio, images, radio, mobile sensors, or other modalities) in mobile and IoT/edge devices. I am particularly interested in the following paradigms:

  • Mobile computing/sensing. Mobility, interactivity, resource-constrained, context awareness or security - what are their limits?
  • On device learning. Resource efficiency, personalized model or interoperability - How to update the model?
  • Transfer learning. How can models generalize out of distribution and efficient predict uncertainties?

Previously, I completed a PhD in Computer Science at the The University of New South Wales working with Prof. Wen Hu.
