Biography

I am a Research Fellow at the University of Melbourne (2024-). Prior to that,I was a Research Associate at the University of Cambridge (2021-2024). I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of New South Wales (UNSW) in 2021.

My research primarily focuses on efficient and on-device machine learning and LLMs, human-centered computing, and pervasive computing. I have served as a PC and reviewer for top-tier journals and conferences, including NeurIPS, IMWUT, CHI, UIST, PMCJ, TMC, IPSN, SenSys, and TheWebConf. I have been recognized for my contributions to the field with several awards, including the 2024 Best Paper Runner-Up Award at ACM IPSN (Core A*), the 2023 ACM MobiSys (Core A*) Rising Star Award from ACM SIGMOBILE, and the 2022 Best Poster Runner-Up Award at ACM MobiCom (Core A*).
