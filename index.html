Hong Jia

Research Fellow
University of Melbourne
hong.jiaunimelb.edu.au
       

I am a Research Fellow (2024-) in the HCI group at the University of Melbourne working with Prof. Vassilis Kostakos. Prior to that I was a Research Associate (2021-2024) at the University of Cambridge working with Prof. Cecilia Mascolo. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science in 2021 from the UNSW. I am a recipient of Australian Government Research Training Program (RTP) Scholar (2017-2021) and ACM MobiSys Rising Star (2023).

My research explores novel mobile sensing capabilities to advance new forms of human-computer interaction (HCI) and deploys edge AI systems to enhance real-time, trustworthy, and efficient interactions in dynamic and real-world environments. Specifically, it includes:

  • HCI. Designing novel sensing modalities and systems to enhance human-centered interactivity, security, localization, and healthcare.
  • Edge AI. Designing novel software and on-device AI algorithm co-design systems to push the boundaries of how AI systems can securely and seamlessly interact with users.
